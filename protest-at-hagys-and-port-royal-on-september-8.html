Protest at Hagys and Port Royal on September 8!

There will be a protest at the corner of Hagys Mill and Port Royal on September 8th at 8am.

The intention is to draw attention to the sale of the 24 acres of native woods.

This protest is being held away from the actual entrance to SCEE, so that there is no interference for parents dropping their kids off to school. Additionally, the protest will be kept at a low volume, and will not block traffic. There will be at least one person in a toad costume.

This is our opportunity to show how the community, current and former staff, philadelphians writ large, and literally anyone with a pulse is against this sale.
